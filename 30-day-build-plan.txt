30-Day Build Plan (Tax + Non-Tax AI Products)

Primary Goal (30 days)
- Launch 2 MVPs (1 tax, 1 non-tax)
- Validate demand with real prospects
- Book sales calls for Chea to close

MVPs to Build
1) Tax Strategy AI Pre-Screen
2) SOP/Workflow AI Builder (non-tax, service businesses)

Team Roles
- Chea: Offer design, positioning, sales calls, closing, final go/no-go
- Cory: Execution management, QA, risk controls, scope discipline
- Zack: Core architecture, coding, app logic
- Anthony: Integrations, workflow automation, systems optimization

Week 1 (Days 1–7): Scope + Assets + Alignment
- Finalize one-line offer promise for each MVP
- Define ICP and top pain points
- Lock must-have v1 features (cut nice-to-haves)
- Build workflow maps (intake -> AI -> output -> delivery)
- Draft intake forms, prompts, output templates
- Create sales one-pager + short demo script
- Internal dry-run specs + QA checklist

Deliverables by end of Week 1
- Signed-off MVP brief (both products)
- Locked scope and role ownership
- Demo-ready messaging and templates

Week 2 (Days 8–14): Build MVPs
- Zack builds core app flows and logic
- Anthony wires automations and integrations
- Cory manages sprint board + QA process
- Chea refines positioning and records demo narrative

Deliverables by end of Week 2
- Working internal MVPs (both)
- 10 sample scenario outputs each
- Prioritized fix list

Week 3 (Days 15–21): Pilot + Outreach
- Run pilots with 5–10 warm prospects per MVP
- Launch outbound campaign (email/DM) by segment
- Capture objections, feature gaps, pricing resistance
- Chea runs calls and closes early pilot accounts

Deliverables by end of Week 3
- 10–20 prospect conversations
- 3–5 pilot users
- Pricing hypothesis v1

Week 4 (Days 22–30): Productize + Scale
- Tighten onboarding and handoff process
- Add billing/onboarding docs
- Build KPI dashboard (leads, demos, close rate, churn risk)
- Finalize repeatable weekly cadence (idea -> build -> sell)

Deliverables by Day 30
- 2 packaged offers
- 1 repeatable outbound campaign
- Revenue-ready operating loop

Success Metrics (30-day)
- MVPs launched: 2
- Pilot users: >=3
- Sales calls booked: >=10
- Initial paid conversions: >=1-3

Operating Rules
- Keep communication short/direct
- Execution over overplanning
- Chea owns closing + final ideation decisions
- Minimize admin overhead
