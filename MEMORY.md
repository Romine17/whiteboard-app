Created: 2026-02-26

### Canonical offer
- Offer name: **Business Owner + High-Income Tax Checklist**
- Audience: **Business owners + high-income individuals earning over $300K/year**
- Pricing hook: **$299 strike-through → FREE (limited-time launch offer)**

### Funnel flow (current)
1. User clicks **Claim Free Checklist** on products/home page.
2. User is routed to **AI intake page** (`/pages/ai-intake.html`).
3. Intake captures:
   - Name, email, annual income
   - Income breakdown: W-2, capital gains, business, retirement
   - Auto-calculated "other" income = annual total - four categories
   - Profile, entity, top goal, timing
4. On completion, user clicks **Book CPA Consultation**.
5. Scheduling goes directly to Calendly (`https://calendly.com/cromine-rhw/30-min-zoom`) with prefilled fields:
   - `a1`: full intake notes
   - `a2`: annual income
   - `a3`: profile
   - `a4`: income breakdown
   - `a5`: entity + goal + timing
