Business Owner + High-Income Tax Checklist (Preview)

Designed for business owners and individuals earning over $300K/year.

Section 1: Income & Entity Snapshot

  • Total projected income this year (W-2, K-1, business, capital gains)
  • Current entity structure (Sole Prop / LLC / S-Corp / C-Corp)
  • Expected major events (sale, bonus, RSU vesting, liquidity event)

Section 2: Tax Reduction Opportunity Scan

  • Entity and owner compensation optimization reviewed
  • Retirement contribution optimization reviewed
  • Family payroll/benefit strategy reviewed (if applicable)
  • Depreciation/capital expenditure strategy reviewed (if applicable)
  • State and SALT impact strategy reviewed

Section 3: Timing & Cashflow Planning

  • Quarterly estimated tax schedule updated
  • Income deferral / acceleration scenarios modeled
  • Deduction timing opportunities identified
  • Year-end action deadlines documented

Section 4: Risk & Documentation

  • Documentation checklist completed for each selected strategy
  • Audit-support file structure established
  • Implementation owners and due dates assigned

Section 5: 90-Day Action Plan

  • Top 3 opportunities selected by impact and feasibility
  • Execution timeline set (30/60/90 days)
  • Next strategy review date scheduled
This checklist is educational and planning-oriented. Strategy suitability depends on individual facts and current tax law.
